❓ Help
What is KHangMan?

A hangman word-guessing game. Guess the hidden word letter by letter before the drawing is complete (10 tries).

What categories are available?

🦁 Animals, 🍕 Food, 🌍 Countries, 🔬 Science. Each has words in all 3 languages.

How does the hint work?

Click 💡 Hint to see a clue about the word (e.g., 'Fast hunting bird' for FALCON).

Does the word list change by language?

Yes. Switching to French gives French words, Arabic gives Arabic words with the Arabic alphabet keyboard.

What letters should I guess first?

In English: E, A, T, N, S are the most common. In French: E, A, S, I, N.

Is there a score?

Yes. Wins vs total games played is tracked throughout your session.

🎯 Learning Goals

Vocabulary building, spelling awareness, letter frequency intuition, deductive reasoning, category knowledge, multilingual awareness.

📚 Word Lists

Animals: 15 EN / 10 FR / 8 AR words. Food: 10/8/6. Countries: 10/8/6. Science: 10/7/5. Each word has a hint.

⌨️ Strategy

English: guess E,A first (most common vowels), then T,N,S (common consonants). Save Q,X,Z for last.

🔗 Based On

KHangMan by KDE Education Project. Supports 20+ languages. Original: apps.kde.org/khangman

💡 Pro Tips

Start with Animals (most familiar). Always guess vowels first. After game over, study the revealed word. Play in Arabic to build Arabic vocabulary.
