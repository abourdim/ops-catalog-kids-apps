❓ Help
What is Circuit Builder?

An interactive simulation where you build electrical circuits by placing components on a grid and connecting them with wires.

Does it work offline?

Yes. Once loaded, everything runs in your browser with no internet needed.

Can I play on my phone?

Yes. Works on any device with a modern browser — phone, tablet, laptop, desktop.

How do I change the theme?

Click any theme button in the controls bar.

How do I change the language?

Click EN / FR / ع. Arabic enables right-to-left layout automatically.

Is my data private?

Yes. Everything runs locally in your browser. No data is sent anywhere.

🎯 Purpose

Learn basic electronics by building circuits. Understand how batteries, wires, switches, bulbs, and resistors work together.

🔋 Battery

Provides electrical energy. Current flows from the + terminal through the circuit and back to the - terminal.

💡 Light Bulb

Converts electrical energy into light. Glows yellow when current flows through it in a complete circuit.

🔘 Switch

Controls the flow of current. When closed, current can pass. When open, the circuit is broken.

📏 Resistor

Limits the flow of current. Used to protect components from too much electricity.

🔗 Series vs Parallel

Series: components in a single path. Parallel: components in branching paths sharing the same start and end points.

🎨 Themes

8 built-in themes: Terminal, Midnight, Ember, Phosphor, Signal, Redshift, Arctic, Sand.

🌐 Languages

Trilingual: English, Français, العربية. Arabic auto-enables RTL layout.

🔊 Sound

Web Audio API — no files needed. Toggle with the speaker button.

🔒 Privacy

Local-first. No tracking, no analytics, no external calls. All data stays in your browser.
