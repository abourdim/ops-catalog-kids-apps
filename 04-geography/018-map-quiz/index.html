❓ Help
What is World Map Quiz?

An interactive geography game! Click on continents and countries on a world map to test your knowledge.

Does it work offline?

Yes. Once loaded, everything runs in your browser with no internet needed.

Can I play on my phone?

Yes. Works on any device with a modern browser. The map scales to fit your screen.

How do I change the theme?

Click any theme button (🖥 🌌 🔥 etc.) in the controls bar.

How do I change the language?

Click EN / FR / ع. Arabic enables right-to-left layout automatically.

What are the difficulty levels?

Easy = 7 continents only. Medium = continents + 10 major countries. Hard = 20 countries from around the world.

Is my data private?

Yes. Everything runs locally in your browser. No data is sent anywhere.

🎯 Purpose

Learn world geography by locating continents and countries on an interactive map.

🌍 Coverage

7 continents and 20 countries: Egypt, Morocco, Turkey, Saudi Arabia, Pakistan, Indonesia, Malaysia, India, China, Japan, France, UK, Germany, USA, Canada, Brazil, Australia, South Africa, Nigeria, Russia.

🎨 Themes

8 built-in themes: Terminal, Midnight, Ember, Phosphor, Signal, Redshift, Arctic, Sand.

🌐 Languages

Trilingual: English, Français, العربية. Arabic auto-enables RTL layout.

🔊 Sound

Web Audio API — no files needed. Correct = rising tone, wrong = buzz, win = melody. Toggle with 🔊 button.

🔒 Privacy

Local-first. No tracking, no analytics, no external calls. All data stays in your browser.
