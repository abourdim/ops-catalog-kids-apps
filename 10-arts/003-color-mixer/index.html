❓ Help
What is Color Mixer?

An interactive art tool to learn color theory! Mix colors freely or solve color-mixing quizzes.

What are the two modes?

Free Mix lets you pick any two colors and see the result. Quiz Mode challenges you to figure out which two colors make a target color.

Does it work offline?

Yes. Once loaded, everything runs in your browser with no internet needed.

Can I play on my phone?

Yes. Works on any device with a modern browser — phone, tablet, laptop, desktop.

How do I change the theme?

Click any theme button in the controls bar.

How do I change the language?

Click EN / FR / ع. Arabic enables right-to-left layout automatically.

Is my data private?

Yes. Everything runs locally in your browser. No data is sent anywhere.

🎯 Purpose

Learn color theory interactively! Discover primary, secondary, tertiary colors, tints, and shades.

🎨 Color Theory

Primary colors (Red, Blue, Yellow) combine to make secondary colors (Orange, Green, Purple). Adding white creates tints; adding black creates shades.

🎨 Themes

8 built-in themes: Terminal, Midnight, Ember, Phosphor, Signal, Redshift, Arctic, Sand.

🌐 Languages

Trilingual: English, Francais, Arabic. Arabic auto-enables RTL layout.

🔊 Sound

Web Audio API — no files needed. Toggle with the sound button.

🔒 Privacy

Local-first. No tracking, no analytics, no external calls. All data stays in your browser.
