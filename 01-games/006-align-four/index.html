❓ Help
What is Align Four?

A strategy game where you drop tokens into a 7×6 grid trying to line up 4 of your color in a row — horizontally, vertically, or diagonally.

How does the AI work?

Tux uses minimax with alpha-beta pruning. Easy = 2 moves ahead, Medium = 4, Hard = 6. Hard is very challenging!

Can I play with a friend?

Yes! Switch to "2 Players" mode. Take turns on the same device.

What's a diagonal win?

Four tokens of your color in a diagonal line (like a staircase going up-left or up-right).

Can I use keyboard?

Yes. Press keys 1-7 to drop in that column. Press R to restart.

🎯 Learning Goals

Strategic thinking, planning ahead, spatial awareness, pattern recognition, anticipating opponent moves.

⚔️ Strategy Tips

Control the center column — it gives the most winning options. Build threats in two directions at once to force your opponent into a losing choice. Look for diagonal setups.

🔗 Based On

GCompris Align Four by Bharath M S. The classic game was invented in 1974 by Howard Wexler. Also known as Connect 4, Four in a Row.

🧠 Did You Know?

Connect 4 was mathematically solved in 1988. The first player can always win with perfect play by starting in the center column!
