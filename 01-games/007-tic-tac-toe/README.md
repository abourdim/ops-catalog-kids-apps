## 🎯 Purpose

The world's most familiar strategy game. Place X or O on a 3×3 grid — first to get three in a row wins. Perfect for introducing strategic thinking to young children.

## 📖 What You'll Learn

- Turn-based strategy — planning your move and reacting to your opponent
- Win/block recognition — seeing three-in-a-row threats
- Game theory basics — the game is "solved" (perfect play = draw)
- Logical deduction — if I go here, they'll go there

## 🎮 How to Play

1. Choose mode: vs Tux or 2 Players
2. Press ▶ New Game
3. Click any empty cell to place your X
4. Tux places O (or Player 2 takes their turn)
5. First to get 3 in a row (horizontal, vertical, diagonal) wins
6. If all 9 cells fill up with no winner — it's a draw

## 💡 Tips

- Always take the center if it's free — it gives the most winning paths
- If the opponent takes a corner, take the center
- With perfect play from both sides, the game always ends in a draw

## 🔗 Based On

**GCompris Tic Tac Toe** — one of the oldest known games, dating back to ancient Egypt (~1300 BC).
