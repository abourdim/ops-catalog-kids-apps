## 🎯 Purpose

Practice arithmetic under time pressure. Comets carrying math equations fall toward penguin igloos. Solve the equation and fire the laser to destroy the comet before it hits.

This isn't a worksheet — it's a defense mission. The boy must think fast, calculate accurately, and protect his penguin base. Speed and accuracy both improve with practice.

---

## 📖 What You'll Learn

- **Addition** — single and double digit (Cadet, Scout)
- **Subtraction** — with borrowing (Scout)
- **Multiplication** — times tables 2–10 (Ranger)
- **Division** — exact division, no remainders (Commander)
- **Mixed operations** — choosing the right operation under pressure (Commander)
- **Negative numbers** — adding negative values (Legend)
- **Mental arithmetic speed** — getting faster with practice
- **Number sense** — estimating answers before calculating
- **Working under pressure** — timed challenges build focus

---

## 🎮 How to Play — Step by Step

1. **Choose your level** from the dropdown (Cadet → Legend)
2. **Press ▶ Play** to start the mission
3. **Watch the sky** — comets fall carrying equations like `7 + 5 = ?`
4. **Type the answer** in the input box using number keys
5. **Press Enter** (or tap 🔥 FIRE) to fire the laser
6. **Correct answer** → laser shoots up and destroys the comet ✓
7. **Wrong answer** → laser fires but misses (try again on the next comet)
8. **Too slow** → comet hits an igloo, damaging it
9. **Each igloo takes 2 hits** — first hit damages, second hit destroys
10. **Lose all 4 igloos** = mission over

## 📊 Difficulty Levels

| Level | Name | Operations | Number Range | Comet Speed |
|-------|------|------------|-------------|-------------|
| 1 | **Cadet** | + only | 1–10 | Slow |
| 2 | **Scout** | + and − | 1–20 | Medium |
| 3 | **Ranger** | × only | 2–10 | Medium |
| 4 | **Commander** | + − × ÷ | 1–100 | Fast |
| 5 | **Legend** | All + negatives | −10 to 100 | Very fast |

---

## 💡 Tips for Parents & Kids

- **Start with Cadet** and master it (100% accuracy) before moving up
- **Focus on accuracy first** — speed comes naturally with practice
- **The lowest comet is the most urgent** — solve it first, it's closest to the igloos
- **Practice multiplication tables separately** before attempting Ranger level
- **Play daily for 10 minutes** — short consistent sessions beat long rare ones
- **Use the Activity Log** to see which equations you got wrong — practice those
- **Arabic mode** shows Arabic-Indic numerals — great for bilingual math practice

## ⌨️ Controls

| Key | Action |
|-----|--------|
| Number keys (0–9) | Type answer |
| Minus key (−) | Type negative number |
| Enter | Fire laser |
| Backspace | Clear answer |

On mobile: use the on-screen input and FIRE button.

---

## 📊 Scoring

- **Points per hit**: 10–50 (more points for hitting comets higher up — quicker answers score more)
- **No penalty for wrong answers** — but you waste time, and comets keep falling
- **Streak tracking** — consecutive correct answers tracked in the log

**TuxMath** (Tux, of Math Command) — originally created by Bill Kendrick in 2001.
