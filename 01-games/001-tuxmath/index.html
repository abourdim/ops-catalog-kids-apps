❓ Help
What is TuxMath?

A math defense game. Comets fall with equations — type the answer to fire a laser and destroy them before they hit the penguin igloos.

How do I fire the laser?

Type the answer in the input box and press Enter or tap the 🔥 FIRE button.

What are the difficulty levels?

Cadet (addition 1-10), Scout (add & subtract 1-20), Ranger (multiplication), Commander (all operations), Legend (negatives).

What happens when a comet hits?

The igloo takes damage. First hit cracks it, second hit destroys it and the penguin walks away. Lose all 4 igloos = game over.

Does it work offline?

Yes. Once loaded, everything runs in your browser. No internet needed.

Can I play on my phone?

Yes. Use the on-screen input and FIRE button. Works on any device with a browser.

🎯 Learning Goals

Addition, subtraction, multiplication, division. Mental arithmetic speed. Number sense and estimation. Working under time pressure.

📊 Scoring

10-50 points per hit. Higher comets (faster answers) score more. Streak tracking in the activity log.

⌨️ Controls

Number keys: type answer. Enter: fire. Minus key: negative numbers. Backspace: clear.

🔗 Based On

TuxMath (Tux, of Math Command) by Bill Kendrick, 2001. Original: github.com/tux4kids/tuxmath

💡 Pro Tips

Focus on accuracy over speed. Target the lowest comet first. Master Cadet before moving up. Practice 10 minutes daily.
