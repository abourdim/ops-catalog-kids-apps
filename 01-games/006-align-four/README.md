## 🎯 Purpose

Classic strategy game on a 7×6 grid. Drop colored tokens into columns — first to line up 4 in a row (horizontal, vertical, or diagonal) wins. Play against Tux's AI or a friend.

## 📖 What You'll Learn

- Strategic thinking and planning multiple moves ahead
- Spatial awareness — tracking horizontal, vertical, and diagonal lines
- Pattern recognition — spotting threats and opportunities
- Anticipating opponent moves — defensive and offensive play

## 🎮 How to Play

1. Choose mode: vs Tux (AI) or 2 Players
2. Choose AI difficulty: Easy / Medium / Hard
3. Press ▶ New Game
4. Click an arrow above a column (or press 1-7) to drop your token
5. Your green token falls to the lowest empty cell
6. Tux (red) plays after you
7. First to line up 4 tokens in any direction wins!

## 📊 Difficulty Levels

| Level | AI Depth | Description |
|-------|----------|-------------|
| Easy | 2 moves ahead | Makes mistakes, good for learning |
| Medium | 4 moves ahead | Decent challenge for most players |
| Hard | 6 moves ahead | Very strong — can you beat it? |

## 💡 Tips

- Control the center column — it connects to the most winning lines
- Build threats in two directions to create a fork
- The first player can always force a win by starting in the center (proven mathematically in 1988)
