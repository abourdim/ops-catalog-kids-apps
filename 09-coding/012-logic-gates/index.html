What is Logic Gates?

An interactive simulator to learn the 6 fundamental logic gates (AND, OR, NOT, XOR, NAND, NOR) that form the building blocks of all computers.

What are the two modes?

Explore lets you pick any gate, toggle inputs, and see the output and truth table live. Quiz challenges you to identify which gate produces a given output.

Does it work offline?

Yes. Once loaded, everything runs in your browser with no internet needed.

Can I play on my phone?

Yes. Works on any device with a modern browser — phone, tablet, laptop, desktop.

How do I change the theme?

Click any theme button in the controls bar.

Is my data private?

Yes. Everything runs locally in your browser. No data is sent anywhere.

AND Gate

Outputs 1 only when BOTH inputs are 1. Symbol: flat left, curved right.

OR Gate

Outputs 1 when AT LEAST ONE input is 1. Symbol: curved left and right.

NOT Gate

Flips the input: 1 becomes 0, 0 becomes 1. Has only one input. Symbol: triangle with circle.

XOR Gate

Outputs 1 when inputs are DIFFERENT. Symbol: like OR but with extra curve on input side.

NAND Gate

Opposite of AND — outputs 0 only when both inputs are 1. Symbol: AND with circle on output.

NOR Gate

Opposite of OR — outputs 1 only when both inputs are 0. Symbol: OR with circle on output.

Themes

8 built-in themes: Terminal, Midnight, Ember, Phosphor, Signal, Redshift, Arctic, Sand.

Sound

Web Audio API — no files needed. Correct = rising tone, wrong = buzz, win = melody. Toggle with sound button.

Privacy

Local-first. No tracking, no analytics, no external calls. All data stays in your browser.
