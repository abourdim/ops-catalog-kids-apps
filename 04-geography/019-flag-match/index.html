❓ Help
What is Flag Match?

A fun geography game where you identify country flags! Each flag is drawn on a canvas and you pick the correct country from 4 choices.

Does it work offline?

Yes. Once loaded, everything runs in your browser with no internet needed.

Can I play on my phone?

Yes. Works on any device with a modern browser — phone, tablet, laptop, desktop.

How do I change the theme?

Click any theme button in the controls bar.

How do I change the language?

Click EN / FR / ع. Arabic enables right-to-left layout automatically.

What do the difficulty levels mean?

Easy = 10 well-known flags. Medium = 20 flags. Hard = 40+ flags from all over the world.

Is my data private?

Yes. Everything runs locally in your browser. No data is sent anywhere.

Purpose

Learn world flags! Match flags to their countries and discover fun facts about each nation.

Themes

8 built-in themes: Terminal, Midnight, Ember, Phosphor, Signal, Redshift, Arctic, Sand.

Languages

Trilingual: English, Francais, العربية. Arabic auto-enables RTL layout.

Sound

Web Audio API — no files needed. Correct = rising tone, wrong = buzz, win = melody. Toggle with the sound button.

Flags

40+ flags drawn programmatically on canvas using simple shapes — rectangles, circles, crescents, stars, and crosses. No images needed!

Privacy

Local-first. No tracking, no analytics, no external calls. All data stays in your browser.
