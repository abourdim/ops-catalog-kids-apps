❓ Help
What is Sorting Visualizer?

An interactive tool to learn how sorting algorithms work by watching or doing the sorting yourself!

What are the two modes?

Watch Mode: The algorithm sorts automatically with animations. Play Mode: You perform the swaps manually following hints.

Does it work offline?

Yes. Once loaded, everything runs in your browser with no internet needed.

Can I play on my phone?

Yes. Works on any device with a modern browser.

How do I change the theme?

Click any theme button in the controls bar.

What do the colors mean?

Yellow = comparing, Red = swapping, Green = sorted/in place, Default = unsorted.

Is my data private?

Yes. Everything runs locally. No data is sent anywhere.

Bubble Sort

Repeatedly walks through the list comparing adjacent pairs and swapping them if out of order. Easy to understand, O(n^2) time.

Selection Sort

Finds the minimum element from the unsorted part and places it at the beginning. Also O(n^2) but does fewer swaps than Bubble Sort.

Insertion Sort

Builds the sorted array one item at a time by inserting each element into its correct position. Efficient for small or nearly-sorted arrays.
