❓ Help
What is Solar System Explorer?

An interactive app to explore planets, learn facts, and test your knowledge about our solar system!

Does it work offline?

Yes. Once loaded, everything runs in your browser with no internet needed.

Can I play on my phone?

Yes. Works on any device with a modern browser.

How do I change the theme?

Click any theme button in the controls bar.

How do I change the language?

Click EN / FR / ع. Arabic enables right-to-left layout automatically.

What are the two modes?

Explore mode lets you click planets to see info cards. Quiz mode asks you to identify planets!

Is my data private?

Yes. Everything runs locally in your browser. No data is sent anywhere.

🎯 Purpose

Learn about the 8 planets, their distances, temperatures, moons, and fun facts through interactive exploration and quizzes.

🔭 Explore Mode

Planets orbit the Sun in real-time animation. Click any planet to see a detailed info card.

❓ Quiz Mode

Three difficulties: Easy (4 inner planets), Medium (all 8), Hard (planets + moons + asteroid belt questions).

🎨 Themes

8 built-in themes: Terminal, Midnight, Ember, Phosphor, Signal, Redshift, Arctic, Sand.

🌐 Languages

Trilingual: English, Français, العربية. Arabic auto-enables RTL layout.

🔊 Sound

Web Audio API. Toggle with the speaker button.

🔒 Privacy

Local-first. No tracking, no analytics, no external calls.
