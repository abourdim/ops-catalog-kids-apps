## 🎯 Purpose

Learn keyboard skills through action gameplay. Fish fall from the sky, each carrying a letter or word. Type the correct key and Tux slides across to eat the fish. Miss it, and the fish splats on the ground.

This is the first step toward touch typing — learning where keys are on the keyboard through rapid, fun repetition rather than boring drills.

---

## 📖 What You'll Learn

- **Letter recognition** — connecting written letters to keyboard keys
- **Keyboard layout** — building muscle memory for key positions
- **Typing speed** — getting faster through gameplay pressure
- **Word recognition** — reading short and long words quickly (Scout, Ranger levels)
- **Spelling awareness** — typing words letter by letter reinforces spelling
- **Hand-eye coordination** — tracking falling objects while typing
- **Focus** — maintaining attention as speed increases

---

## 🎮 How to Play — Step by Step

1. **Choose your level** from the dropdown:
   - **Cadet** — single letters fall (A, B, C...)
   - **Scout** — short 3-letter words (CAT, DOG, SUN...)
   - **Ranger** — long 5-7 letter words (PLANET, ROCKET, CASTLE...)
2. **Press ▶ Play** to start
3. **Fish appear** at the top of the screen with a letter or word on them
4. **Type the letter** (or the first letter of the word) on your keyboard
5. **Tux slides** to the fish and eats it — crunch! 🐟
6. **For words** (Scout/Ranger): type each letter in sequence to complete the word
7. **If a fish hits the ground**, you lose a life (shown as fish icons, top right)
8. **Lose all 5 lives** = game over

## 📊 Difficulty Levels

| Level | Name | Content | Fish Speed | Max Fish |
|-------|------|---------|------------|----------|
| 1 | **Cadet** | Single letters A–Z | Slow | 6 |
| 2 | **Scout** | 3-letter words (CAT, DOG, RUN...) | Medium | 6 |
| 3 | **Ranger** | 5–7 letter words (PLANET, CASTLE...) | Fast | 6 |

---

## 💡 Tips for Parents & Kids

- **Start with Cadet** — don't rush to words until single letters are comfortable
- **Look at the screen, not the keyboard** — this builds real typing skill
- **When multiple fish fall**, target the lowest one first (closest to the ground)
- **For words**, once you start typing a word, finish it — don't switch mid-word
- **10 minutes daily** is better than 1 hour once a week
- **Pair with KTouch or Klavaro** for structured lesson-based typing practice
- **Play after TuxMath** for a change of pace — same falling mechanic, different skill

## ⌨️ Controls

| Key | Action |
|-----|--------|
| A–Z keys | Type the letter / next letter in word |
| (no Enter needed) | Letters register immediately |

On mobile: this game works best with a physical keyboard. Bluetooth keyboards work on tablets.

---

## 📊 Scoring

- **Points per fish**: word length × 10 (single letters = 10, "PLANET" = 60)
- **Accuracy tracked**: caught vs missed in the activity log
- **Best streak**: consecutive catches without a miss
