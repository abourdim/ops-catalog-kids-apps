❓ Help
What is Word Search?

Find hidden words in a grid of letters! Words can be placed horizontally, vertically, or diagonally depending on difficulty.

How do I select a word?

Click or tap the first letter, then drag to the last letter of the word. Release to confirm your selection.

Does it work offline?

Yes. Once loaded, everything runs in your browser with no internet needed.

Can I play on my phone?

Yes. Works on any device with a modern browser — phone, tablet, laptop, desktop.

How do I change the theme?

Click any theme button in the controls bar.

How do I change the language?

Click EN / FR / ع. Arabic enables right-to-left layout automatically.

Is my data private?

Yes. Everything runs locally in your browser. No data is sent anywhere.

Purpose

Find hidden words in a letter grid. Great for vocabulary building and pattern recognition.

Difficulty

Easy: 8x8 grid, horizontal only. Medium: 10x10, horizontal + vertical. Hard: 12x12, all directions including diagonal.

Categories

8 categories: Animals, Colors, Fruits, School, Nature, Space, Body, Sports. Each has 8-12 words.

Themes

8 built-in themes: Terminal, Midnight, Ember, Phosphor, Signal, Redshift, Arctic, Sand.

Languages

Trilingual: English, Francais, Arabic. Arabic auto-enables RTL layout.

Sound

Web Audio API — no files needed. Found = rising tone, wrong = buzz, win = melody. Toggle with sound button.

Privacy

Local-first. No tracking, no analytics, no external calls. All data stays in your browser.
