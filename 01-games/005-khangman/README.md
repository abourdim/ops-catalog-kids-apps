## 🎯 Purpose

Build vocabulary, spelling awareness, and deductive thinking. A hidden word is shown as blank slots. Guess one letter at a time — correct guesses reveal the letter in its position, wrong guesses draw the hangman figure. You have 10 tries.

This isn't just a guessing game — it's a vocabulary builder. Every word comes with a hint, and playing across categories (Animals, Food, Countries, Science) expands knowledge while training spelling and letter-frequency intuition.

---

## 📖 What You'll Learn

- **Vocabulary** — exposure to new words across 4 categories in 3 languages
- **Spelling** — seeing words letter by letter reinforces correct spelling
- **Letter frequency** — learning which letters are common (E, A, T, S...) builds language intuition
- **Deductive reasoning** — using revealed letters to narrow down the word
- **Category knowledge** — animals, food, geography, and science facts through hints
- **Risk management** — choosing between safe common letters and risky specific guesses
- **Multilingual awareness** — play in English, French, or Arabic with native word lists

## 🎮 How to Play — Step by Step

1. **Choose a category** from the dropdown:
   - 🦁 **Animals** — FALCON, DOLPHIN, CAMEL, EAGLE...
   - 🍕 **Food** — PIZZA, MANGO, HONEY, DATES...
   - 🌍 **Countries** — EGYPT, JAPAN, MOROCCO, BRAZIL...
   - 🔬 **Science** — GRAVITY, PLANET, MAGNET, OXYGEN...
2. **Press ▶ New Word** to start
3. **See the blank slots** — each slot is one letter of the hidden word
4. **Click a letter** on the on-screen keyboard (or press it on your physical keyboard)
5. **Correct letter** → it appears in all matching positions, key turns green ✓
6. **Wrong letter** → one part of the hangman is drawn, key turns grey ✗
7. **Use the 💡 Hint button** if you're stuck — it shows a clue about the word
8. **Guess all letters** before 10 wrong guesses to win 🏆
9. **10 wrong guesses** = game over, the word is revealed 💀

## 📚 Word Categories

### 🦁 Animals (15 words per language)
Examples: FALCON, DOLPHIN, CAMEL, EAGLE, PANTHER, COBRA, SHARK, WOLF, TIGER

Hints describe the animal's key feature: "Fast hunting bird", "Desert traveller", "Ocean apex predator"

### 🍕 Food (10 words per language)
Examples: BURGER, PIZZA, MANGO, RICE, HONEY, OLIVE, DATES, CHEESE

Hints describe the food: "Sweet bee product", "Mediterranean fruit for oil"

### 🌍 Countries (10 words per language)
Examples: BRAZIL, EGYPT, JAPAN, MOROCCO, TURKEY, CANADA, INDIA

Hints describe geography: "Land of the pyramids", "North African kingdom"

### 🔬 Science (10 words per language)
Examples: GRAVITY, PLANET, MAGNET, OXYGEN, CRYSTAL, PRISM, PHOTON

Hints describe the concept: "Force that pulls things down", "Splits light into colors"

## 💡 Tips for Parents & Kids

- **Start with Animals** — most familiar category for children
- **Always guess vowels first**: E, A, I, O, U (in English) — they appear in almost every word
- **Common consonants** to try early: T, N, S, R, L
- **Use the hint** — there's no shame in it, and you learn the word's meaning
- **After a game over**, look at the revealed word and try to memorize it
- **Science category** is the hardest but teaches the most interesting vocabulary
- **Play in Arabic** to practice the Arabic alphabet and build Arabic vocabulary
- **Countries category** pairs well with KGeography — learn the name, then find it on a map
- **Keep score** — the app tracks wins vs total games played

## 📊 Strategy Guide

**Letters to guess first (English):**

| Priority | Letters | Why |
|----------|---------|-----|
| 1st | E, A | Most common vowels in English |
| 2nd | T, N, S | Most common consonants |
| 3rd | R, I, O | Frequently appearing letters |
| 4th | L, H, D | Common in animal/country names |
| Last | Q, X, Z, J | Rare letters — only guess if you're sure |

**Letters to guess first (French):**
E, A, S, I, N, T, R, L

**Letters to guess first (Arabic):**
ا, ل, ن, ر, م, ي, و
