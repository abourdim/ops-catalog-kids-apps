❓ Help
What is Fish Cascade?

A typing game where fish fall from the sky with letters or words. Type the correct key to make Tux catch and eat the fish.

How do I play?

Just press the letter key shown on the falling fish. For words (Scout/Ranger), type each letter in sequence.

What are the levels?

Cadet: single letters A-Z. Scout: short 3-letter words. Ranger: long 5-7 letter words.

What happens if I miss a fish?

It hits the ground and you lose a life. You have 5 lives (shown as fish icons).

Does it teach real typing?

Yes! It builds keyboard familiarity through fun repetition — the same method used by typing tutors worldwide.

Can I play on a tablet?

Best with a physical keyboard. Bluetooth keyboards work on tablets.

🎯 Learning Goals

Letter recognition, keyboard layout, typing speed, word recognition, spelling awareness, hand-eye coordination.

📊 Scoring

Points = word length × 10. Single letter = 10 points, PLANET = 60 points. Accuracy and best streak tracked.

⌨️ Controls

A-Z keys only. Letters register immediately — no Enter needed.

🔗 Based On

TuxType (Fish Cascade mode) by Tux4Kids. Original: github.com/tux4kids/tuxtype

💡 Pro Tips

Look at the screen, not the keyboard. Target the lowest fish first. Start with Cadet until all letters are comfortable.
