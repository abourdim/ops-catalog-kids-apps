❓ Help
What is Pixel Art?

A creative drawing tool where you paint individual pixels on a grid to create art, just like coloring squares on graph paper!

Does it work offline?

Yes. Once loaded, everything runs in your browser with no internet needed.

Can I play on my phone?

Yes. Works on any device with a modern browser — phone, tablet, laptop, desktop. Touch and drag to paint!

How do I change the theme?

Click any theme button (🖥 🌌 🔥 etc.) in the controls bar.

How do I change the language?

Click EN / FR / ع. Arabic enables right-to-left layout automatically.

What are templates?

Pre-made outlines (star, heart, cat, etc.) shown as light gray guides on the grid. Pick one from the dropdown to get started!

Is my data private?

Yes. Everything runs locally in your browser. No data is sent anywhere.

🎨 Purpose

Create pixel art masterpieces! Paint individual pixels on a grid to make characters, scenes, patterns, and more.

🖌 Tools

Paint: click/drag to color pixels. Fill: flood-fill a connected area. Erase: remove pixel color. Clear: reset the entire grid.

🎨 Themes

8 built-in themes: Terminal, Midnight, Ember, Phosphor, Signal, Redshift, Arctic, Sand.

🌐 Languages

Trilingual: English, Français, العربية. Arabic auto-enables RTL layout.

🔊 Sound

Web Audio API — no files needed. Paint sounds, fill sounds, and clear sounds. Toggle with the sound button.

🔒 Privacy

Local-first. No tracking, no analytics, no external calls. All data stays in your browser.
